The Best Movies According to Ahmad

My top 3 movies of all-time

The Terminator

"The Terminator" is a classic science fiction film directed by James Cameron, released in 1984. Set in a dystopian future, it follows the story of a cyborg assassin, played by Arnold Schwarzenegger, sent back in time to kill Sarah Connor, the mother of the future leader of the human resistance against machines. The movie explores themes of artificial intelligence, time travel, and the struggle for survival in a post-apocalyptic world.

Venom

"Venom" is a superhero film based on the Marvel Comics character of the same name, released in 2018. The story follows journalist Eddie Brock, played by Tom Hardy, who becomes host to an alien symbiote, granting him superhuman abilities. As Eddie learns to control the symbiote, he must confront other symbiotes and a sinister corporation while navigating his newfound powers and responsibilities.

Deadpool

"Deadpool" is a comedic superhero film released in 2016, starring Ryan Reynolds as the titular character. Known for its irreverent humor and breaking the fourth wall, the movie follows Wade Wilson, a wisecracking mercenary who undergoes an experimental procedure to cure his cancer but ends up with accelerated healing powers. As Deadpool, he seeks revenge on the man who disfigured him while also trying to protect his loved ones.
